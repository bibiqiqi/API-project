What is foraging?

Foraging is the act of looking for wild plants and fungi, that are edible.

...and why should I care?

  • Wild edibles tend to be higher in mineral and nutrient content than cultivated edibles
  • It's a fun way to get excercise and experience the benefits of being outdoors
  • You'll be developing your "natural literacy"
